Top 10 Tourist Destinations in the Philippines

A one-page visual guide to ten unforgettable places across the archipelago, from powdery beaches and coral lagoons to heritage towns and mountain terraces.

01

El Nido, Palawan

Limestone cliffs, clear lagoons, and island-hopping routes make El Nido one of the country's most iconic tropical escapes.

02

Boracay

Famous for White Beach, Boracay blends soft sand, sunset sails, water sports, and lively evenings in one compact island.

03

Banaue Rice Terraces

These ancient Ifugao terraces sweep across the Cordillera mountains in dramatic layers of green.

04

Chocolate Hills, Bohol

More than a thousand rounded hills create a surreal landscape that turns chocolate-brown during the dry season.

05

Mayon Volcano, Albay

Mayon's near-perfect cone dominates the Albay skyline, best paired with Cagsawa ruins, ATV trails, and clear morning views.

06

Kawasan Falls, Cebu

Bright turquoise pools, jungle trails, and canyoneering routes make Kawasan a signature Cebu adventure.

07

Siargao

A surf capital with island lagoons, coconut roads, tidal pools, and a relaxed coastal rhythm.

08

Vigan, Ilocos Sur

Cobblestone streets, Spanish-era houses, and horse-drawn calesas give Vigan its preserved heritage-town atmosphere.

09

Batanes

Windswept hills, stone houses, quiet roads, and dramatic sea views make Batanes feel beautifully remote.

10

Intramuros, Manila

The walled city offers Manila's colonial history through stone fortifications, churches, museums, plazas, and walkable lanes.
